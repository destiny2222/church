ABOUT US

Egan Ministries is a living proof of the faithfulness of God, the validity of the liberation mandate and the spirit of God at work in this commission.

In the past couple of years, the lord has launched us into new chapters. With diverse testimonies of changed lives and the undeniable presence of God in our midst, the membership strength has multiplied and soon, we will be commencing with our second service.
