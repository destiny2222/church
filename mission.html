MISSIONS

Egan Ministries is making an eternal difference locally and globally. Below are the global missions we support on a monthly basis.

Vision

Our vision is bigger than just us. At Grace we are bringing light into some of the darkest places on earth.

  • “Then Jesus came to them and said, “All authority in heaven and on earth has been given to me. Therefore go and make disciples of all nations, baptizing them in the name of the Father and of the Son and of the Holy Spirit, and teaching them to obey everything I have commanded you. And surely I am with you always, to the very end of the age.” Matthew 28:18-20

Arms of Love

Arms of Love is a ministry in Bishkek, Kyrgyzstan dedicated to helping abused women and children of all ages. They provide food, clothing, and shelter as well as life skills training. Most importantly, the ministry is giving eternal hope through sharing the love of Jesus.

Egan Ministries

We are a global family, we preach prosperity through faith in our works for God. We live by faith, be Known by Love, & be a Voice of Hope.
